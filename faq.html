Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our Products page, add meals to your cart, and complete checkout online.

Do you deliver?

Yes! We deliver to most areas in Cape Town and are expanding to new regions soon.

Can I customize my meals?

Absolutely. You can request substitutions or add-ons when placing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and secure online payment options.

Is MealZoom healthy?

Our meals are designed to balance nutrition and taste, with options for fitness and wellness goals.
